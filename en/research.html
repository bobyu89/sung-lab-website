Cognitive Training & Dementia Care

Cognition MCI Dementia

Multi-domain cognitive-training modules that improve cognition in older adults with mild cognitive impairment and mild dementia.

More details

Research Question

Can multi-domain cognitive training improve working memory, attention, executive function, and overall cognition in older adults?

Representative Studies

  • One-year prospective randomized controlled trial (Journal of Global Health, 2023)
  • Meta-analysis of cognitive training on attention domains (Journal of Global Health, 2023)
  • Network meta-analyses of emotion-oriented therapies and multisensory stimulation in dementia

Methods

RCT, Meta-analysis, Network meta-analysis

Clinical Impact

Replicable cognitive-training modules for older adults with MCI and mild dementia.

Swallowing Health & Geriatric Rehabilitation

Dysphagia Frailty Rehabilitation

Tongue-resistance training and evidence-based swallowing rehabilitation that protect eating safety and quality of life in frail older adults.

More details

Research Question

Can tongue-resistance and strengthening training improve tongue strength and oropharyngeal swallowing in frail older adults?

Representative Studies

  • Double-blind RCTs (Journal of Oral Rehabilitation 2025; GeroScience 2025; Journal of Global Health 2025)
  • Network meta-analysis of swallowing rehabilitation therapies (GeroScience 2024)
  • IOPI tongue-pressure assessment as a predictor of dysphagia (BMC Geriatrics 2025)

Methods

Double-blind RCT, Network meta-analysis, IOPI instrument assessment

Clinical Impact

Swallowing-training programs for frail elders in community and institutional care.
